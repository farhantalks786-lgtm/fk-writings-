An Orphan

By Farhan | October 30, 2025

The school bell rings,

Parents arrive, smiling bright,

Holding their beloved children tight.

But at the back bench, one boy remains,

Gazing out through the window panes.

No one notices,

No one even turns to see.

He longs for love, for warmth, for care —

But from where?

His heart’s dearest ones now lie beneath the soil,

God called them home before their toil.

Once, he was just a boy with dreams,

Now the world knows him by one word —

“Orphan.”
